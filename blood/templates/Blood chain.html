Blood Chain


Blood chain refers to the receipients and donors of the different blood groups. In other words, we can say that a person can recieve blood from which different blood group categories or donate blood to which persons having different blood group. It is like a chain by which we can receive or donate blood. For example, 'A+' blood group can receive blood from 'O-', 'O+', 'A-' and 'A+'; but it can donate blood to 'A+' and 'A-' blood groups only.

  • 'AB+' blood group is a universal receipient.
  • 'O-' blood group is a universal donor.
